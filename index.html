Financing

Sharia-Compliant Financing Structures

Trade and asset finance

Murabaha

Used for equipment, stock, vehicles, and business assets through transparent cost-plus financing.

Best for: Members who need asset-backed purchases with clear repayment terms.
Profit-sharing partnership

Mudaraba

Sahal SACCO provides capital while the entrepreneur manages the venture and shares profit on agreed ratios.

Best for: Experienced entrepreneurs with strong business capability and growth plans.
Joint venture finance

Musharaka

Both the SACCO and the member contribute capital to a venture and share results proportionally.

Best for: Members seeking partnership-based financing for projects or property.
Lease-based financing

Ijara

Assets are acquired by the SACCO and leased to the member, including lease-to-own options where suitable.

Best for: Machinery, property, and productive assets that support long-term growth.
Operational liquidity

Working Capital

Short-term support for inventory cycles, cash flow timing, and seasonal business operations.

Best for: Traders and SMEs that need responsive financing for daily activity.
Governance and protection

Independent Sharia Review

Products, contracts, and audits are supervised to keep financing consistent with halal principles and cooperative standards.

Key benefit: Members receive financing with stronger ethical assurance and transparency.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sahal SACCO licensed and regulated?

Yes. Sahal SACCO is licensed and operates under national cooperative law and Islamic financial principles. Our independent Sharia Supervisory Board conducts quarterly AAOIFI-standard audits.

Are my savings Sharia-compliant?

Yes. All savings are invested in halal activities only. Profits are shared under a Mudaraba profit-sharing model â€” absolutely no interest (riba).

Can I transfer my membership shares?

Yes. Membership shares may be transferred in accordance with the SACCO's policies and Islamic principles. Contact Member Services for the transfer process.

Can I withdraw my savings at any time?

Yes. Members may withdraw savings in accordance with SACCO guidelines, ensuring fairness and Sharia compliance. Kaydsi savings offers the most flexible withdrawal terms.

How long does it take to become a member?

Membership is typically approved within 3 business days after completing required documentation and meeting the minimum share purchase requirement.

Do I need an existing bank account to join?

No. 75% of our members had no prior formal banking relationship. We welcome all community members who wish to save and invest ethically.
